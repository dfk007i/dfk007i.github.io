Scalable Chat Systems

Building chat systems that can handle large volumes of users requires careful consideration of infrastructure and scalability. This includes choosing appropriate technologies, such as message queuing and distributed databases, and designing systems that can handle peak loads without compromising performance.

Newer Clouds - Fly

The cloud landscape is constantly evolving, and new platforms like Fly are emerging as viable alternatives to established players like AWS and Google Cloud. Fly offers a unique approach to cloud hosting, focusing on simplicity and developer experience. It’s worth exploring newer cloud platforms like Fly to see if they can provide a better fit for your needs.

Autoscaling Backends

As your application grows and attracts more users, you’ll need to ensure that your backend is able to scale to meet demand. This can be achieved through autoscaling, which automatically adjusts the number of server instances based on traffic patterns. Implementing autoscaling can help you avoid downtime and ensure that your application remains responsive even during peak periods.

NFT Mints, DDoS Protection

The rise of NFTs has brought new challenges, such as DDoS attacks aimed at disrupting NFT minting processes. Implementing DDoS protection measures is crucial for ensuring a smooth and secure minting experience for users.

Web3 Wallet Checkpoint

The security of Web3 wallets is essential for protecting users’ digital assets. Regularly checking your Web3 wallet for vulnerabilities and keeping it updated with the latest security patches is important to minimize the risk of theft.

Rust

Rust is a relatively new programming language that is gaining popularity for its focus on memory safety and performance. Rust is well-suited for building high-performance, secure applications, making it a valuable tool for developers working on blockchain and Web3 projects.

Building an Exchange, Finance Stuff

Building a cryptocurrency exchange or other financial application requires a deep understanding of financial regulations and compliance requirements. It’s essential to consult with legal and financial experts to ensure your project is compliant with all applicable laws.

Compliance, KYC Stuff

Know Your Customer (KYC) regulations require businesses to verify the identity of their customers. Implementing KYC procedures is essential for preventing financial crime and fraud.

React Native

React Native is a popular framework for building cross-platform mobile applications. It allows developers to use their existing JavaScript skills to build apps for both iOS and Android, saving time and resources.

Blockchain Sweepers/Indexers

Blockchain sweepers are tools used to identify and claim unclaimed or abandoned cryptocurrency. Blockchain indexers provide a way to search and analyze data stored on blockchains.

Monitoring Systems

Effective monitoring is crucial for ensuring the health and performance of your applications. Implementing monitoring systems allows you to identify and address issues quickly before they impact users.
